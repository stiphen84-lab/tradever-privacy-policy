Privacy Policy for TradeVer

This document describes how TradeVer handles your information.

This Privacy Policy describes how TradeVer ("App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and protects the information of users (each, a "User") of the TradeVer mobile application (the "Service"). TradeVer is a free-to-use virtual trading simulator designed exclusively for entertainment and educational purposes, offering a simulated "real trading experience" without any financial risk or real monetary transactions.

1. Data We Collect and How We Collect It

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our Service. The types of data collected fall into three main categories:

A. Personal Data (Contact Information - Collected with Consent)

  • Data Collected: Names and phone numbers associated with your device contacts.
  • Purpose of Collection: This permission is requested solely to enable optional social features within the simulator, such as inviting specific friends to use the app, joining friend-based leagues, or participating in private virtual competitions.
  • Use of Data: Contact data is used only for facilitating these invitation or social interaction features. We do not use this contact information for advertising or sell this data to third parties.
  • Opt-Out: Granting this permission is optional and can be revoked at any time through your device's settings.

B. App Usage and Simulated Trading Data (Non-Personal)

  • Data Collected: Simulated portfolio performance, virtual transaction history, login times, duration of sessions, feature usage (e.g., watchlists created, simulator settings used).
  • Purpose: To provide core Service functionality, track User engagement, and improve the quality and realism of the virtual trading experience.

C. Device and Technical Information (Log Data)

  • Data Collected: IP address, unique device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID), device type, operating system version, and crash data.
  • Purpose: To diagnose technical issues, monitor Service stability, understand application usage trends, and ensure the app is functioning correctly.

2. How We Use the Collected Information

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate the virtual trading simulator, authenticate users, and manage virtual accounts.
  • For Analytics and Improvement: To analyze user behavior and measure the effectiveness of the simulator.
  • For Security and Compliance: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity.
  • For Communication: To send push notifications (with user consent) related to virtual market activity or app updates.
  • For Advertising: To show advertisements within the app, as detailed in Section 3.

3. Third-Party Service Providers and Advertising

Advertising

TradeVer contains advertisements delivered by third-party advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob).

  • Data Sharing for Ads: These third parties may collect and use information about your device, including identifiers (like the Advertising ID), and usage activity (excluding your Contacts data) to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements.
  • Disclaimer: We do not control the data collection practices of these advertising partners. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Service Providers

We use third-party tools for functions like analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics) and crash reporting. These parties are restricted from using your information for any purpose other than providing services on our behalf.
